Detailed Description

Solve constrained system by solving original mixed system; see ConstrainedSolver.

Solves the saddle-point problem with a block-diagonal preconditioner, with user-provided preconditioner in the top-left block and (by default) an identity matrix in the bottom-right.

This is the most general ConstrainedSolver, needing only Operator objects to function. But in general it is not very efficient or scalable.
