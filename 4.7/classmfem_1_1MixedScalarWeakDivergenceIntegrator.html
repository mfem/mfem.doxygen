Detailed Description

Class for integrating the bilinear form \(a(u,v) := (-\hat{V} u, \nabla \cdot v)\) in 2D or 3D and where \(\hat{V}\) is a vector coefficient, \(u\) is in \(H^1\) or \(L_2\) and \(v\) is in \(H^1\).
