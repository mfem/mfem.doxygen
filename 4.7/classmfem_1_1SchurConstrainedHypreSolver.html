Detailed Description

Basic saddle-point solver with assembled blocks (ie, the operators are assembled HypreParMatrix objects.)

This uses a block-diagonal preconditioner that approximates \( [ A^{-1} 0; 0 (B A^{-1} B^T)^{-1} ] \).

In the top-left block, we approximate \( A^{-1} \) with HypreBoomerAMG. In the bottom-right, we approximate \( A^{-1} \) with the inverse of the diagonal of \( A \), assemble \( B diag(A)^{-1} B^T \), and use HypreBoomerAMG on that assembled matrix.
