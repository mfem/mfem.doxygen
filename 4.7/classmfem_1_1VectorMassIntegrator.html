Detailed Description

Class for integrating the bilinear form \(a(u,v) := (Q u, v)\), where \(u=(u_1,\dots,u_n)\) and \(v=(v_1,\dots,v_n)\), \(u_i\) and \(v_i\) are defined by scalar FE through standard transformation.
